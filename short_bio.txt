Zhengliang Shi is currently (2025.07.24) a second-year Master's student in Computer Science and Technology at the General Artificial Intelligence Lab at Shandong University. His primary research focuses on Large Language Models (LLMs).
He has gained valuable experience through multiple internships, including at Baidu and Tencent, as well as through academic visits to Leiden University and University of Amsterdam in the Netherlands.

In addition to his research, he serves as an Area Chair for ARR (ACL Rolling Review) and review for top-tier conferences and journals such as SIGIR, WWW, WSDM, and IPM. Outside of academics, he enjoys staying active and engaged in a variety of hobbies, including running, fitness, and reading.
